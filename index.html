About Me

Hello! My name is Kioni Pablo and I am originally from the island of Guam! I have been living in Seattle for about three and a half years. Prior to living in Seattle, I was living in San Diego and was there for four and a half years. During my time in San Diego, I was attending the University of San Diego where I received my Bachelor's Degree in Accounting. As soon as I graduated from the University of San Diego, I relocated to Seattle where I worked as an Accountant at a company called SSA Marine for 2 years. After my time at SSA Marine, I found a job as an Accountant at Zillow where I currently am at now.

Currently, I am looking to transition my career to Software Engineering. I would like to use my Accounting background to get into a Software Engineering position that focuses on the development of Financial Applications.

Outside of working at Zillow and furthering my knowledge in coding at the University of Washington, I actively train and compete in the sport of Jui Jitsu at Gracie Barra (Seattle). I love to travel to new places, experience different cultures, and try new foods. So far I have been to Greece, Thailand, Japan, Philippines, and several different states in the U.S.
